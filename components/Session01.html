DSA
  • What ?
    • Combination of Data Structure and Algorithms
    • DS : Efficient data management to improve storage and retrieval
    • Algorithm: Set of instructions to solve a particular problem
  • Why should we learn ?
    • A building block of Software Development
    • Helps us develop memory and time effecient application/software
    • Helps us shape our thought process - try to optimize our solution, suggest alternatives
    • Similar process is being followed in Software Development
    • Popular interview Standard
  • How ?
    • Consistency is the key, practice daily!!
    • Ensure that you solve problem on you own, do not copy-paste from internet.
    • Ok to read / understand the solution, but please implement it on your own after that.
